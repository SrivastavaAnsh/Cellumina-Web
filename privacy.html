Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

We operate the Cellumina application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

Cellumina is designed to help users learn cellular biology through interactive simulations. We prioritize your privacy and aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

Personal Data: We do not ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) while you use Cellumina.

Usage Data and Device Information: We may collect non-personal information automatically when you use the app, such as:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App crash reports and usage statistics (via Apple's opt-in analytics)

This data is completely anonymous and is used solely to improve the performance and stability of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

The anonymous data we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.
  • To fix bugs, crashes, and other technical issues.
  • To understand how users interact with the simulations to create better educational content.

3. Local Storage

The App uses local storage (such as Apple's UserDefaults or @AppStorage) to save your progress, such as whether you have completed the onboarding screen, your interactions, and your preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. If you delete the app, this local data may be deleted as well.

4. Third-Party Services

Cellumina does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising networks. The only data collected is what you explicitly opt-in to share with Apple via iOS crash reporting and App Analytics. You can manage these settings directly in your device's Settings app under Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

5. AI Features and Apple Intelligence

Cellumina features an integrated AI Tutor powered directly by Apple's native Foundation Models framework. All AI processing is handled locally on your device or securely through Apple's infrastructure. We do not use third-party AI APIs, and we do not log, record, or store any of your interactions with the AI tutor on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Cellumina is an educational app that can be used by children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because the App does not require user registration or internet connectivity to function, no child's personal information is collected by us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
